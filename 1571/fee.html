PRIMARY

The Tuition fees for june and the first term must be paid before the end of May. This applives even to those who are seeking financial assistance from the school. Those in receipt of Government Concessions are promoted to the next higher class need not pay fees.

Fees will be paid thorugh the South Indian Bank. Fee bills will be available online which parents can print form the website and pay to the bank by cash.
